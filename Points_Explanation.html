Game points — rules & examples

Assume you bet 7 out of 10 points
  • Points double for correct bets. Example: 7 bet points = 14 points if correct
  • Points are lost for incorrect bets. Example: 7 bet points = 0 points if incorrect
  • Points that are not bet earn 5%. Example: 3 points not bet = 3.15 points
Example of total points earned for a bet of 7 out of 10 points
  • If your bet is correct: 14 points + 3.15 points = 17.15 points
  • If your bet is incorrect: 0 points + 3.15 points = 3.15 points
Compensation
  • At the end of the game, each point earned will be converted to a bonus worth half a cent ($0.005)
